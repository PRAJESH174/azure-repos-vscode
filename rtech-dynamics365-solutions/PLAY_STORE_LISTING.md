## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: #2563eb (Blue 600)
- **Secondary**: #1e40af (Blue 800)
- **Accent**: #3b82f6 (Blue 500)
- **Background**: #ffffff (White)
- **Text**: #1f2937 (Gray 900)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, Roboto, or SF Pro (Bold)
- **Body**: Inter, Roboto, or SF Pro (Regular)
- **Code**: Fira Code, JetBrains Mono, or Courier

### Screenshot Design Tips
1. Use actual app screenshots, not mockups
2. Add subtle captions/annotations to highlight features
3. Show real content, not lorem ipsum
4. Maintain consistent branding across all images
5. Use high-quality, crisp images
6. Show the app in action, not just static screens
7. Include diverse use cases
8. Demonstrate key differentiators
